📚 The Mammu Dictionary: Key Terms

To truly understand Mammu, you need to know the lingo. Here are the essentials:

Kundan (കുന്ദൻ): A special title for someone who has perfected the art of being hilariously annoying. It's a term of endearment for a person who can get on your nerves and make you laugh in the same breath.
Myren (മൈരൻ): When used between friends, this is a badge of honor. It signifies a lovable rascal, a clever scoundrel, and the kind of unpredictable character who makes life interesting.
